Garden Whisperers Club

We are a comunity of people who love to garden and help each other.

-Grandma Sheryl

We've exchanged so many seeds and plants and advice... We have so much fun gardening and even doing other activities since we became such good friends. It's so nice to see clubs like these exist nowdays.
